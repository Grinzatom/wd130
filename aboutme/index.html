About Me

Upon learning about BYU pathway, I was charmed and captivated by its method of education, mainly teaching one another as part of the methodology to improve learning. It has helped me, along with other techniques, to even improve my performance at work and in my studies.

An advantage that I also really like when migrating professions is the flexibility of working in this area even from home, allowing me to better manage my time to care for my family and, in turn, my calling in the church.

I am truly grateful for the opportunity to return to what I like and studying with you is something that from the beginning has changed and improved my life and that of my family by having a broader and totally different vision. Thank you and I hope to see you soon in class.
