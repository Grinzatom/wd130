about Me

Hello, my name is Mauricio Grinzato, I have 16 years of experience immersed in the world of electronics. This is my passion and I really want everyone to know how wonderful the world of electronics and telecommunications is. I have been an instrumentation and electronics technician since 2007. Since that time I have evolved and worked in multiple areas of electronics with PLC automation and robotics. This project aims to show you that it is possible, regardless of your age and origin, that you can learn and grow in this world and I am here to demonstrate with you that you can.

The arduino for children project

Electronics for children with Arduino represents a summary of our technical experience adapted to be taught to people of any age and level of academic preparation and anywhere in the world. We invite you to participate and see for yourself that you can become an expert from scratch if you put your mind to it and we want to see you grow and be a new member of our community.
